What is a fraction?

A fraction is a part of a whole, and consists of a number divided by another number. The numerator, the top number, is considered the part of a whole. The denominator, the bottom number, is considered the whole.

Examples include 1/8 and 13/5. In these examples, 1 and 13 are numerators, while 8 and 5 are the denominators. In these examples, 1/8 represents 1 out of 8 equal pieces that create a whole and 13/5 represents 13 pieces out of 5 equal pieces that create a whole.
